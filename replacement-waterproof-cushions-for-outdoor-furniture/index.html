Fabric: Choose a fabric that is both waterproof and fade-resistant.

The fabric of your replacement waterproof cushions is one of the most important factors to consider. You want to choose a fabric that is both waterproof and fade-resistant, so that your cushions will last for many years to come.

  • Polyester: Polyester is a synthetic fabric that is both waterproof and fade-resistant. It is also relatively inexpensive, making it a good choice for budget-minded shoppers.
  • Acrylic: Acrylic is another synthetic fabric that is waterproof and fade-resistant. It is more expensive than polyester, but it is also more durable.
  • Sunbrella: Sunbrella is a brand of acrylic fabric that is specifically designed for outdoor use. It is waterproof, fade-resistant, and mildew-resistant, making it a great choice for replacement waterproof cushions.
  • Canvas: Canvas is a natural fabric that is waterproof and fade-resistant when treated with a water-repellent finish. It is a more expensive option than polyester or acrylic, but it is also more durable.

Once you have chosen a fabric, you can start shopping for replacement waterproof cushions. Be sure to read the product descriptions carefully to make sure that the cushions are made from the fabric that you want.
