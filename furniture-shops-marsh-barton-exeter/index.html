FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about garden furniture, along with their answers, to help you make informed decisions when shopping for outdoor furniture in Marsh Barton, Exeter:

Question 1: What materials are commonly used for garden furniture?

Answer 1: Garden furniture is typically made from a variety of materials, including wood, metal, plastic, and wicker. Wood is a classic choice for its natural beauty and durability, while metal is known for its strength and weather resistance. Plastic is a popular option for its affordability and low maintenance, and wicker adds a touch of traditional charm to any outdoor space.

Question 2: How do I choose the right size garden furniture for my space?

Answer 2: When selecting garden furniture, it's important to consider the size of your outdoor space and the number of people you typically entertain. Choose pieces that are proportionate to the size of your patio or garden, and avoid overcrowding the area. Measure your space before you shop, and consider the layout of your outdoor area to ensure that there's enough room for movement and activities.

Question 3: What features should I look for in garden furniture?

Answer 3: Look for garden furniture that is comfortable, durable, and weather-resistant. Consider features such as adjustable seating, cushions for added comfort, and UV protection to prevent fading. If you have a small space, consider foldable or stackable furniture that can be easily stored away when not in use.

Question 4: How do I care for my garden furniture?

Answer 4: Regular maintenance is essential to keep your garden furniture looking its best and lasting for years to come. Clean your furniture regularly with a mild detergent and water, and apply protective treatments or sealants as recommended by the manufacturer. Store your furniture indoors or cover it during harsh weather conditions to protect it from the elements.

Question 5: Where can I find quality garden furniture in Marsh Barton, Exeter?

Answer 5: Marsh Barton is home to a diverse range of furniture shops, including several reputable stores that specialize in garden furniture. You'll find a wide selection of styles, materials, and price ranges to suit your needs and preferences. Be sure to visit multiple shops to compare prices and find the best deals.

Question 6: Can I mix and match different styles of garden furniture?

Answer 6: Mixing and matching different styles of garden furniture can be a great way to create a unique and personalized outdoor space. However, it's important to choose pieces that complement each other in terms of color, material, and overall design. Consider creating a cohesive look by incorporating common elements, such as a color scheme or a particular material, throughout your furniture choices.
