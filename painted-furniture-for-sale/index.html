� to Care For

Painted furniture is incredibly easy to care for. This is because the paint finish creates a protective barrier that helps to resist scratches and stains.

  • Clean with a damp cloth: To clean painted furniture, simply wipe it down with a damp cloth. You can use a mild soap solution if necessary, but be sure to rinse the furniture thoroughly afterwards.
  • Avoid harsh chemicals: Do not use harsh chemicals or abrasive cleaners on painted furniture, as these can damage the finish.
  • Protect from heat and sunlight: Avoid placing painted furniture in direct sunlight or near heat sources, as this can cause the paint to fade or crack.
  • Touch up scratches: If your painted furniture gets scratched, you can easily touch it up with a matching paint color. Simply apply a small amount of paint to the scratch and allow it to dry.

By following these simple care tips, you can keep your painted furniture looking its best for years to come.
