FAQ

Have questions about garden furniture? We've got you covered with our comprehensive FAQ section.

Question 1: What materials are best for garden furniture?
Answer: The best materials for garden furniture are those that are durable, weather-resistant, and easy to maintain. Some popular options include teak, acacia, and aluminum.

Question 2: How do I choose the right size garden furniture?
Answer: When choosing the right size garden furniture, consider the size of your outdoor space and the number of people you typically entertain. You'll also want to make sure there's enough room to move around comfortably.

Question 3: What style of garden furniture is right for me?
Answer: The style of garden furniture you choose should complement your personal taste and the overall design of your outdoor space. Consider traditional, contemporary, or eclectic styles.

Question 4: How do I care for my garden furniture?
Answer: To keep your garden furniture looking its best, regular cleaning and maintenance are essential. Use mild soap and water to clean the furniture, and apply teak oil or a similar protectant to wooden furniture to prevent fading and cracking.

Question 5: Can I leave my garden furniture outside all year round?
Answer: Whether or not you can leave your garden furniture outside all year round depends on the climate you live in and the materials your furniture is made from. If you live in a harsh climate, it's best to store your furniture indoors during the winter months.

Question 6: How do I assemble my garden furniture?
Answer: Most garden furniture comes with clear instructions for assembly. Be sure to follow the instructions carefully and use the appropriate tools to ensure proper assembly.
