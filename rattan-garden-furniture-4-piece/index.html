Material: Rattan is a durable and weather-resistant material, but there are different types of rattan.

Rattan is a durable and weather-resistant material, making it an ideal choice for outdoor furniture. However, there are different types of rattan, each with its own unique characteristics.

  • Natural rattan: Natural rattan is the most traditional type of rattan, and it is made from the stems of the rattan palm. Natural rattan is durable and weather-resistant, but it can be more expensive than other types of rattan.
  • Synthetic rattan: Synthetic rattan is made from polyethylene or PVC, and it is designed to look like natural rattan. Synthetic rattan is less expensive than natural rattan, and it is also more durable and weather-resistant.
  • Polyethylene rattan: Polyethylene rattan is a type of synthetic rattan that is made from high-density polyethylene (HDPE). Polyethylene rattan is very durable and weather-resistant, and it is also UV-resistant, meaning that it will not fade in the sun.
  • PVC rattan: PVC rattan is a type of synthetic rattan that is made from polyvinyl chloride (PVC). PVC rattan is less durable and weather-resistant than polyethylene rattan, but it is also less expensive.

When choosing a rattan garden furniture 4-piece set, it is important to consider the different types of rattan and choose the type that is best suited to your needs.
