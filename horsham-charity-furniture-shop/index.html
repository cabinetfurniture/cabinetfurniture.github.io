Two decades of service

The Horsham Charity Furniture Shop stands as a testament to the enduring spirit of community and the power of collective action. For over two decades, the shop has been a pillar of support for local families, providing essential furniture, homeware, and accessories while simultaneously generating funds for vital community initiatives.

The shop's journey began in the early 2000s when a group of dedicated volunteers recognized the need for affordable furniture and the opportunity to make a positive impact on the community. With unwavering determination, they established the Horsham Charity Furniture Shop, a modest venture that quickly blossomed into a thriving social enterprise.

Over the years, the shop has evolved and grown, expanding its selection of donated items and forging strong partnerships with local organizations. Horsham Matters charity

The Horsham Charity Furniture Shop is an integral part of the larger Horsham Matters charity, a registered charity dedicated to improving the lives of individuals and families in the local community. Established in 1998, Horsham Matters encompasses a wide range of initiatives and services aimed at fostering a more compassionate and inclusive society.

  • Community support:
    Horsham Matters provides a lifeline of support to those facing financial difficulties, offering grants and assistance to help cover essential expenses such as rent, utility bills, and food. The charity also runs a food bank, distributing food parcels to those in need.
  • Homeless outreach:
    Horsham Matters works tirelessly to address the issue of homelessness in the community. The charity provides temporary accommodation and support services to those without a permanent home, helping them find long-term housing solutions and overcome the challenges of homelessness.
  • Employment and training:
    Recognizing the importance of financial independence, Horsham Matters offers employment and training opportunities to individuals facing barriers to employment. The charity runs a social enterprise, a training café, and a variety of other initiatives aimed at helping people gain the skills and experience needed to secure meaningful work.
  • Social inclusion:
    Horsham Matters strives to create a sense of belonging and inclusion for all members of the community. The charity organizes regular community events, runs social clubs, and provides support groups for individuals experiencing isolation or other challenges.

The Horsham Charity Furniture Shop plays a crucial role in supporting the work of Horsham Matters.
