Durable and long-lasting

Perspex furniture is renowned for its exceptional durability and longevity, making it an ideal choice for both indoor and outdoor applications.

  • Resistant to scratches and abrasions: Perspex is a highly scratch-resistant material, which means that it can withstand daily wear and tear without losing its smooth and glossy finish.
  • Impact-resistant: Perspex is also impact-resistant, making it less susceptible to damage from accidental bumps or knocks. This makes it a great choice for families with children or for use in high-traffic areas.
  • Weather-resistant: Perspex is unaffected by UV rays and moisture, making it suitable for both indoor and outdoor use. It will not fade, crack, or warp when exposed to sunlight or rain, ensuring its longevity even in harsh weather conditions.
  • Easy to clean and maintain: Perspex furniture is non-porous and easy to clean, requiring only a simple wipe-down with a damp cloth and mild detergent. Its smooth surface prevents dirt and grime from accumulating, making it a hygienic and low-maintenance choice.

Due to its exceptional durability and ease of maintenance, perspex furniture is an investment that will last for many years to come, providing both functionality and aesthetic appeal to your living space.

Weather-resistant

Perspex furniture's exceptional weather resistance makes it an ideal choice for both indoor and outdoor applications. Unlike traditional materials such as wood or metal, perspex is unaffected by UV rays and moisture, ensuring its longevity even in harsh weather conditions.

Here's a closer look at how perspex furniture withstands the elements:

UV resistance: Perspex contains UV inhibitors that protect it from the sun's harmful rays. This prevents the material from fading, yellowing, or becoming brittle over time, ensuring that your furniture retains its vibrant colors and glossy finish.

Moisture resistance: Perspex is non-porous and waterproof, making it highly resistant to moisture damage. It will not absorb water or swell when exposed to rain, snow, or humidity. This makes perspex furniture an excellent choice for outdoor use, as it can withstand even the most extreme weather conditions.

Temperature resistance: Perspex has a wide temperature tolerance range, making it suitable for use in both hot and cold climates. It will not warp or crack when exposed to high temperatures and will remain durable even in freezing conditions.

Due to its exceptional weather resistance, perspex furniture is an ideal choice for a variety of outdoor applications, including patio furniture, poolside seating, and outdoor dining sets. It is also a great option for indoor use in areas that are prone to moisture, such as bathrooms, kitchens, and mudrooms.

Optically clear

Perspex furniture is renowned for its exceptional optical clarity, which means that it allows light to pass through it with minimal distortion. This property makes perspex an ideal choice for a variety of applications, from transparent chairs and tables to decorative accessories and lighting fixtures.

Here's a closer look at how perspex furniture achieves its optical clarity:

High light transmission: Perspex has a very high light transmission rate, allowing up to 92% of light to pass through it. This makes perspex furniture appear almost invisible, creating a sense of openness and spaciousness in any room.

Low refractive index: Perspex has a low refractive index, which means that it does not bend light significantly. This results in minimal distortion, ensuring that objects viewed through perspex furniture appear clear and undistorted.

Clarity and transparency: Perspex is known for its exceptional clarity and transparency, allowing you to see through it with ease. This makes it an excellent choice for display cases, shelves, and other applications where visibility is important.

Due to its optical clarity, perspex furniture can be used to create a variety of unique and stylish effects. For example, transparent chairs can give the illusion of floating, while perspex tabletops can create the appearance of a seamless surface. Perspex can also be used to create decorative accessories, such as vases and sculptures, that add a touch of elegance and modernity to any space.

Easy to clean and maintain

Perspex furniture is renowned for its ease of cleaning and maintenance, making it a practical choice for busy households and commercial spaces alike.

  • Resistant to chemicals: Perspex is resistant to most household chemicals, including bleach and ammonia. This makes it easy to clean and disinfect perspex furniture without damaging its surface.
  • Simple cleaning routine: To clean perspex furniture, simply wipe it down with a soft cloth and a mild detergent or glass cleaner. Avoid using abrasive cleaners or sponges, as these can scratch the surface.
  • Low maintenance: Perspex furniture requires minimal maintenance. Unlike wood or metal furniture, it does not need to be oiled, waxed, or polished. Simply wipe it down occasionally to keep it looking its best.

Due to its easy cleaning and maintenance, perspex furniture is an ideal choice for families with children or pets. It is also a great option for commercial spaces, such as offices, restaurants, and healthcare facilities, where hygiene and cleanliness are important.

Recyclable and eco-friendly

Perspex furniture is not only durable and stylish, but it is also recyclable and eco-friendly. Perspex is a thermoplastic material, which means that it can be melted down and reused to create new products. This reduces waste and helps to conserve natural resources.
