One of the best things about furniture protecting cloths is that they are machine-washable, making them incredibly easy to clean. This is especially important if you have pets or children, as spills and accidents are bound to happen.

    If you spill something on your furniture, it's important to clean it up as soon as possible to prevent the stain from setting. Machine-washing your furniture protecting cloth is the quickest and easiest way to do this.
