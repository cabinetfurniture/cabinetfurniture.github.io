Choose durable materials

When choosing garden furniture Norfolk, durability is key. You want pieces that can withstand the elements and last for many seasons to come. Here are a few of the most durable materials to consider:

  • Teak

    Teak is a naturally durable hardwood that is resistant to rot, decay, and insects. It is also UV-resistant, so it won't fade or discolor in the sun. Teak furniture is more expensive than other types of wood, but it is worth the investment if you want furniture that will last for many years.

  • Acacia

    Acacia is another durable hardwood that is resistant to rot and decay. It is also a popular choice for garden furniture because it is relatively affordable. Acacia furniture is not as UV-resistant as teak, so it may fade or discolor over time. However, it can be treated with a UV protectant to help extend its lifespan.

  • Plastic

    Plastic garden furniture is a great option if you are looking for something that is durable and affordable. Plastic furniture is also easy to clean and maintain. However, it is important to choose high-quality plastic furniture that is UV-resistant. Otherwise, it may become brittle and crack over time.

  • Metal

    Metal garden furniture is another durable option. Metal furniture is strong and sturdy, and it can withstand the elements well. However, metal furniture can be heavy and it may rust over time. To prevent rust, choose metal furniture that is powder-coated or galvanized.

Once you have chosen a durable material for your garden furniture, you can start to think about style and design. With so many different options to choose from, you are sure to find the perfect pieces to create your dream outdoor space.

Select comfortable pieces

When choosing garden furniture Norfolk, comfort is key. You want pieces that you can relax in and enjoy for hours on end. Here are a few things to consider when selecting comfortable garden furniture:

  • Choose the right cushions. Cushions are essential for making garden furniture comfortable. Choose cushions that are thick and supportive, and that are made from a durable fabric that can withstand the elements.
  • Consider the height of the furniture. The height of your garden furniture will affect how comfortable it is to sit in. Choose furniture that is the right height for you and your guests.
  • Make sure the furniture is sturdy. You don't want your garden furniture to collapse under you when you sit down. Choose furniture that is made from durable materials and that is sturdy enough to support your weight.
  • Add some accessories. Accessories like throws and pillows can make your garden furniture even more comfortable. Choose accessories that are made from soft, comfortable fabrics and that complement the style of your furniture.

By following these tips, you can choose garden furniture Norfolk that is both comfortable and stylish. You'll be able to relax and enjoy your outdoor space for years to come.
