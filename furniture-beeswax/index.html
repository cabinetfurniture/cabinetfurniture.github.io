Question 1: Can I use beeswax to finish any type of garden furniture?

Question 2: How do I prepare my garden furniture for a beeswax finish?
Answer: Before applying beeswax, it is important to clean and dry your garden furniture. You can use a mild soap and water solution to clean the furniture, and then allow it to dry completely.

Question 3: How do I apply beeswax to my garden furniture?
Answer: Beeswax can be applied to garden furniture using a brush, cloth, or sponge. Apply a thin, even coat of beeswax to the furniture, and then allow it to dry for several hours.

Answer: Beeswax should be reapplied to garden furniture every few months, or more often if the furniture is exposed to the elements.
