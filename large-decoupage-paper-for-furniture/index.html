Durable and long-lasting

Large decoupage paper for furniture is made from high-quality materials that are designed to withstand everyday use and wear. Unlike traditional decoupage paper, which can tear easily, large decoupage paper is thick and durable, making it less susceptible to rips and tears.

  • Resistant to fading and discoloration:

    The colors and patterns on large decoupage paper are printed using high-quality inks that are resistant to fading and discoloration. This means that your furniture will look vibrant and new for years to come.

  • Waterproof and moisture-resistant:

    Large decoupage paper is coated with a protective layer that makes it waterproof and moisture-resistant. This means that you can use it on furniture that is exposed to spills, humidity, or condensation without worrying about damage.

  • Easy to clean and maintain:

    Large decoupage paper is easy to clean and maintain. Simply wipe it down with a damp cloth to remove dirt and dust. You can also use a mild soap and water solution to clean tougher stains.

  • Can be applied to a variety of surfaces:

    Large decoupage paper can be applied to a variety of surfaces, including wood, metal, glass, and plastic. This makes it a versatile option for updating furniture of all types.

Because of its durability and longevity, large decoupage paper is a great investment for your furniture. It will help your furniture look its best for years to come.

Easy to apply and remove

Large decoupage paper for furniture is easy to apply and remove, making it a great option for both beginners and experienced crafters. Here are the steps on how to apply large decoupage paper to furniture:

  • Clean the surface of the furniture:

    Before applying the decoupage paper, make sure to clean the surface of the furniture to remove any dirt or dust. This will help the decoupage paper to adhere properly.

  • Measure and cut the decoupage paper:

    Measure and cut the decoupage paper to the desired size. Make sure to leave a few inches of overlap around the edges of the furniture.

  • Apply a layer of decoupage medium to the surface of the furniture:

    Using a brush or sponge, apply a layer of decoupage medium to the surface of the furniture. This will help the decoupage paper to stick to the furniture.

  • Place the decoupage paper on the furniture:

    Carefully place the decoupage paper on the furniture, starting from the center and working your way outwards. Smooth out any wrinkles or bubbles as you go.

  • Apply a layer of decoupage medium over the decoupage paper:

    Once the decoupage paper is in place, apply a layer of decoupage medium over the top. This will help to seal the decoupage paper and protect it from wear and tear.

To remove the decoupage paper, simply peel it off the furniture. The decoupage medium will come off with the paper, leaving the surface of the furniture undamaged.

  • Wood:

    Large decoupage paper can be applied to wood surfaces, such as tables, chairs, dressers, and cabinets. It is a great way to update old or outdated wood furniture and give it a new look.

  • Metal:

  • Glass:

    Large decoupage paper can be applied to glass surfaces, such as glass tables, shelves, and mirrors. It is a great way to add a decorative touch to glass furniture and make it more unique.

  • Plastic:

    Large decoupage paper can also be applied to plastic surfaces, such as plastic chairs, tables, and storage bins. It is a great way to update old or outdated plastic furniture and give it a new look.

The versatility of large decoupage paper makes it a great option for updating furniture of all types. It is a great way to add your own personal style to your furniture and make it unique.

Endless design possibilities

  • Use a single pattern to create a cohesive look:

    Choose a large decoupage paper with a pattern that you love and apply it to all of your furniture. This will create a cohesive look that will make your furniture stand out.

  • Mix and match different patterns to create a eclectic look:

    If you can't decide on just one pattern, mix and match different patterns to create a eclectic look. This is a great way to add your own personal style to your furniture and make it unique.

    If you have a large piece of furniture, such as a dresser or a cabinet, you can use decoupage paper to create a mural. This is a great way to add a unique and personal touch to your furniture.

  • Use decoupage paper to add a touch of color to your furniture:

Environmentally friendly alternative to reupholstering

Reupholstering furniture can be a time-consuming and expensive process. It can also be harmful to the environment, as it often involves the use of harsh chemicals and the disposal of old fabric.

Large decoupage paper for furniture is a more environmentally friendly alternative to reupholstering. It is made from recycled paper and is printed with eco-friendly inks. It is also easy to apply and remove, so you can change the look of your furniture without having to worry about damaging it.

Here are some of the environmental benefits of using large decoupage paper for furniture:

  • Reduces waste: Reupholstering furniture requires the use of new fabric, which can end up in landfills. Large decoupage paper is made from recycled paper, so it helps to reduce waste.
  • Reduces the use of harsh chemicals: Reupholstering furniture often involves the use of harsh chemicals, such as solvents and adhesives. Large decoupage paper is printed with eco-friendly inks, so it does not release harmful chemicals into the environment.
  • Saves energy: Reupholstering furniture requires a lot of energy, as it involves the use of power tools and machinery. Large decoupage paper is easy to apply and remove, so it does not require the use of any energy.

If you are looking for an environmentally friendly way to update your furniture, large decoupage paper is a great option. It is affordable, easy to use, and helps to reduce waste, pollution, and energy consumption.

Question: Can I use large decoupage paper on any type of garden furniture?
Answer: Yes, large decoupage paper can be used on any type of garden furniture, including wood, metal, plastic, and wicker.

Question: Is large decoupage paper waterproof?
Answer: Yes, large decoupage paper is waterproof and can be used outdoors without worry.

Question: How do I apply large decoupage paper to my garden furniture?
Answer: To apply large decoupage paper to your garden furniture, you will need to clean the surface of the furniture, apply a layer of decoupage medium, and then apply the decoupage paper. Once the decoupage paper is applied, you will need to apply another layer of decoupage medium to seal it.

Question: How do I remove large decoupage paper from my garden furniture?
Answer: To remove large decoupage paper from your garden furniture, simply peel it off. The decoupage medium will come off with the paper, leaving the surface of the furniture undamaged.

Question: Can I use large decoupage paper on cushions?
Answer: Yes, you can use large decoupage paper on cushions. However, you will need to use a fabric medium instead of a decoupage medium.

Question: Where can I buy large decoupage paper?
