Easy DIY project, no special skills needed

Applying decals furniture is an incredibly easy DIY project that anyone can do, regardless of their skill level. No special tools or skills are required, and the process is simple and straightforward.

Preparation:

Before you begin, make sure the surface of your furniture is clean and dry. Remove any dirt, dust, or grease to ensure that the decals adhere properly. If the surface is particularly glossy, you may need to lightly sand it to create a better surface for the decals to stick to.

Application:

Once the surface is prepared, you can begin applying the decals. Simply peel off the backing of the decal and carefully place it on the desired surface. Use a credit card or squeegee to smooth out the decal and remove any air bubbles. If you make a mistake, you can easily peel off the decal and reposition it.

Tips for applying decals:

  • Work slowly and carefully to avoid wrinkles or bubbles.
  • If you are applying a large decal, it may be helpful to have someone assist you.
  • If you are applying decals to a curved surface, use a hair dryer to gently heat the decal and make it more pliable.
  • Once the decals are applied, press them firmly into place to ensure that they adhere properly.

Removal:

When you want to remove the decals, simply peel them off slowly and carefully. Decals are typically removable without leaving any residue, but if there is any residue, you can use a mild cleaning solution to remove it.
