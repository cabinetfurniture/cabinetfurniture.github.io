Inspect handles carefully for any damage or wear.

Before you purchase vintage furniture handles, it's important to inspect them carefully for any damage or wear. This is especially important if you're buying the handles online, as you won't be able to see them in person before you buy them.

  • Look for any cracks or chips in the metal. These could be a sign that the handles have been damaged in the past, and they could make the handles more likely to break in the future.
  • Check for any rust or corrosion. This could be a sign that the handles have been exposed to moisture, and it could make the handles more likely to deteriorate over time.
  • Examine the finish of the handles. If the finish is scratched or peeling, it could make the handles look less attractive and it could make them more susceptible to damage.
  • Test the handles to make sure they work properly. Open and close the handles several times to make sure that they move smoothly and that they don't stick or bind.

By inspecting the handles carefully before you purchase them, you can avoid buying handles that are damaged or worn. This will help you to ensure that your new handles will last for many years to come.
