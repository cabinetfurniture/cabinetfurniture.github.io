May warp or crack if not properly cared for

One of the biggest cons of mango wood furniture is that it may warp or crack if not properly cared for. This is because mango wood is a natural material that is susceptible to changes in temperature and humidity. If the wood is not properly sealed and protected, it can absorb moisture from the air, which can cause it to warp or crack.

  • Susceptible to changes in temperature and humidity

    Mango wood is susceptible to changes in temperature and humidity. If the wood is exposed to extreme heat or cold, or if the humidity levels fluctuate too much, it can cause the wood to warp or crack.

  • Needs to be properly sealed and protected

    To prevent mango wood furniture from warping or cracking, it is important to properly seal and protect the wood. This can be done by applying a sealant or finish to the wood. The sealant or finish will help to protect the wood from moisture and other environmental factors.

  • Regular maintenance

    In addition to sealing and protecting the wood, it is also important to regularly maintain your mango wood furniture. This includes dusting and cleaning the furniture regularly, and avoiding placing it in direct sunlight or near heat sources.

If you properly care for your mango wood furniture, it will last for many years to come. However, if you do not properly care for the furniture, it may warp or crack.
