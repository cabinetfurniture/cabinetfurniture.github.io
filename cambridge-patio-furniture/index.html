Answer 1: The best material for garden furniture depends on your needs and preferences. Wood, metal, and plastic are all popular choices. Plastic is lightweight and easy to care for, but it may not be as durable as other materials.

Answer 2: When choosing garden furniture, it's important to consider the size of your space and the number of people you typically entertain. If you're just looking for a place to relax and enjoy your garden, you can choose smaller, more intimate pieces.

Question 3: What are some tips for maintaining garden furniture?
Answer 3: To keep your garden furniture looking its best, it's important to clean it regularly. You can use a mild soap and water solution to clean most types of garden furniture. You should also store your garden furniture in a dry place during the off-season to protect it from the elements.
