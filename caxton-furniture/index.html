The Ultimate Guide to Caxton Furniture

Written by Petter Aug 05, 2025 · 10 min read

For over a century, Caxton Furniture has been a trusted source for high-quality furniture that combines comfort and style. Whether you're looking for living room sofas, dining room tables, or bedroom sets, Caxton has something to suit every taste and need. This comprehensive guide will provide you with everything you need to know about Caxton Furniture, from its history and craftsmanship to its wide range of products and exceptional customer service.

Founded in 1912 in Grand Rapids, Michigan, the heart of the American furniture industry, Caxton Furniture is steeped in a rich tradition of craftsmanship and innovation. The company's skilled artisans use only the finest materials and employ time-honored techniques to create furniture pieces that are both beautiful and durable. Caxton is renowned for its commitment to sustainability, using eco-friendly practices and sourcing materials from responsibly managed forests.

With a wide range of styles to choose from, Caxton Furniture caters to diverse interior design tastes. From classic traditional pieces to modern contemporary designs, Caxton's collections offer something for every room and aesthetic preference. The company also provides customization options, allowing you to tailor your furniture to your specific requirements and create a truly unique living space.

Uncompromising Quality

Caxton Furniture's commitment to quality is evident in every aspect of its products. The company uses only the finest materials, sourced from sustainable forests and suppliers who adhere to strict environmental standards.

  • Durable Construction: Caxton's furniture frames are made from kiln-dried hardwood, ensuring strength and longevity. Joints are reinforced with corner blocks and dowels for added stability.
  • Premium Fabrics and Leathers: Caxton uses high-quality fabrics and top-grain leathers that are resistant to wear and fading. The company offers a wide selection of colors and textures to complement any décor.
  • Expert Upholstery: Caxton's skilled upholsterers take pride in their craftsmanship, ensuring that each piece is tailored to perfection. Cushions are filled with premium materials for maximum comfort and support.
  • Rigorous Testing: Caxton's furniture undergoes rigorous testing to meet industry standards for durability, safety, and performance. This ensures that every piece is built to last and withstand the demands of everyday use.

Caxton Furniture's unwavering commitment to quality has earned the company a reputation for excellence. Customers can trust that they are investing in furniture that will provide years of comfort, style, and enjoyment.

Wide Range of Styles

Caxton Furniture caters to diverse interior design tastes by offering a wide range of styles, from classic traditional to modern contemporary. Whether you prefer the timeless elegance of traditional furniture or the sleek lines of modern design, Caxton has something to suit your needs.

Traditional Styles: Caxton's traditional furniture collections draw inspiration from classic European and American designs. These pieces are characterized by intricate carvings, rich fabrics, and warm finishes. They exude a sense of timeless sophistication and elegance.

Contemporary Styles: Caxton's contemporary furniture collections embrace clean lines, geometric shapes, and neutral colors. These pieces are designed to create a modern and inviting living space. They are perfect for those who appreciate simplicity, functionality, and style.

Transitional Styles: Caxton's transitional furniture collections bridge the gap between traditional and contemporary styles. These pieces combine elements from both worlds, creating a versatile and timeless look. They are ideal for those who want the best of both worlds.

In addition to these core styles, Caxton also offers a variety of specialty collections, such as coastal, farmhouse, and rustic. This ensures that there is something for every taste and décor preference.

Customization Options

  • Finish Options: Caxton offers a variety of finish options for its wood furniture, including stains, paints, and distressing techniques. This allows you to customize the look of your furniture to match your existing décor or create a completely new look.

With Caxton Furniture's customization options, you can create furniture that is truly unique and reflects your personal style. Their experienced design consultants are always available to assist you in making the perfect choices for your home.

Sustainable Practices

Caxton Furniture is committed to sustainability and environmental responsibility. The company believes that it is important to protect the planet for future generations while also providing beautiful and durable furniture for its customers.

Sustainable Materials: Caxton uses sustainable materials whenever possible, such as wood from responsibly managed forests and recycled materials. The company also sources its materials from suppliers who adhere to strict environmental standards.

Eco-Friendly Finishes: Caxton uses low-VOC finishes and adhesives to minimize the environmental impact of its products. These finishes are non-toxic and safe for both people and the environment.

Waste Reduction: Caxton implements waste reduction programs throughout its manufacturing process. The company recycles and reuses materials whenever possible, and it has partnered with organizations to find sustainable solutions for waste disposal.

Energy Efficiency: Caxton's manufacturing facilities are designed to be energy efficient. The company uses energy-efficient lighting and equipment, and it takes steps to reduce its carbon footprint.

Tips

Here are a few tips to help you choose the perfect garden furniture for your outdoor space:

Tip 1: Consider your needs. What will you be using your garden furniture for? Dining, relaxing, or entertaining? Once you know how you'll be using your furniture, you can start to narrow down your choices.

Tip 2: Choose the right materials. Garden furniture is made from a variety of materials, each with its own advantages and disadvantages. Consider the climate you live in, how much maintenance you're willing to do, and your budget when choosing materials.

Tip 3: Measure your space. Before you start shopping for garden furniture, measure the space where you'll be placing it. This will help you avoid buying furniture that's too big or too small.

Tip 4: Consider your style. Your garden furniture should complement the style of your home and outdoor space. If you have a traditional home, you might want to choose classic furniture made from natural materials. If you have a more modern home, you might prefer contemporary furniture made from metal or plastic.

Tip 5: Don't forget the accessories. Cushions, pillows, and throws can add comfort and style to your garden furniture. They can also be used to change the look of your furniture from season to season.

By following these tips, you can choose the perfect garden furniture to create a beautiful and inviting outdoor space that you'll enjoy for years to come.
