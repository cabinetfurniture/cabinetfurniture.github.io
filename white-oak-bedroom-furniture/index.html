Question 1: What's the best material for garden furniture?
Answer: The best material for garden furniture depends on your needs and preferences. Teak, acacia, and eucalyptus are popular choices for their durability and resistance to rot and decay. Question 2: How do I protect my garden furniture from the elements?
Answer: To protect your garden furniture from the elements, you can use furniture covers, sealants, or stains. You should also store your furniture indoors or under a covered area during the winter months.

Question 3: How do I clean my garden furniture?
Answer: The best way to clean your garden furniture depends on the material. For teak furniture, you can use a teak cleaner or a mild soap and water solution. For plastic furniture, you can use a mild soap and water solution or a plastic cleaner. For metal furniture, you can use a metal cleaner or a mild soap and water solution.
