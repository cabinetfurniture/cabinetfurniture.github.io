Easy to apply

One of the best things about lime washing furniture is that it's incredibly easy to apply. You don't need any special skills or tools, and you can do it yourself in just a few hours.

  • Mix the lime wash. The first step is to mix the lime wash. You can either buy pre-mixed lime wash or make your own by mixing equal parts lime powder and water.
  • Apply the lime wash. Once the lime wash is mixed, you can apply it to your furniture using a brush or a sponge. Be sure to apply it in thin, even coats.
  • Let the lime wash dry. Once you've applied the lime wash, let it dry completely. This may take several hours or even overnight.
  • Distress the lime wash. Once the lime wash is dry, you can distress it to give it an aged and weathered look. You can do this by sanding it lightly with sandpaper or by using a wire brush.
