Traditional Techniques

Mouse Man furniture is crafted using traditional techniques that have been passed down through generations of skilled artisans.

One of the most distinctive features of Mouse Man furniture is the use of dovetail joints. These joints are created by interlocking two pieces of wood with a series of precisely cut pins and tails. Dovetail joints are incredibly strong and durable, ensuring that Mouse Man furniture will withstand the test of time.

In addition to dovetail joints, Mouse Man furniture also features traditional mortise and tenon joints. These joints are created by inserting a tenon (a protruding piece of wood) into a mortise (a hole). Mortise and tenon joints are also very strong and durable, and they add a touch of rustic charm to Mouse Man furniture.

The artisans who create Mouse Man furniture take great pride in their work, and they use only the finest materials and techniques. As a result, Mouse Man furniture is renowned for its exceptional quality and craftsmanship.

Family-Run Business

Mouse Man furniture is a family-run business that has been creating beautiful and timeless furniture for over 60 years.

The company was founded in 1959 by Robert "Mouseman" Thompson, a renowned woodcarver and furniture maker. Thompson's unique style of carving, which features charming scenes of rural life and whimsical creatures, quickly became popular, and Mouse Man furniture soon became a sought-after commodity.

Today, Mouse Man furniture is still handcrafted by skilled artisans using the same traditional techniques that Robert Thompson used. The company is now run by Robert Thompson's son, Edward, and his grandson, Tom. Edward and Tom are both passionate about continuing their father's and grandfather's legacy of creating beautiful and durable furniture.

The family-run nature of Mouse Man furniture is one of the things that makes it so special. Each piece is made with care and attention to detail, and the company is committed to using the finest materials and traditional techniques.

When you choose Mouse Man furniture, you are not only investing in a beautiful piece of furniture, but you are also supporting a family-run business that is dedicated to creating timeless and durable pieces.
