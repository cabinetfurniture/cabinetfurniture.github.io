Choose the right type of foam for your needs.

When choosing foam for garden furniture, there are a few things you need to consider: * **The density of the foam:** Density is measured in pounds per cubic foot (PCF). The higher the density, the firmer the foam will be. For garden furniture, you'll want to choose a foam with a density of at least 1.5 PCF. * **The firmness of the foam:** Firmness is measured on a scale of 1 to 10, with 1 being the softest and 10 being the firmest. For garden furniture, you'll want to choose a foam with a firmness of around 5 or 6. This will provide a good balance of comfort and support. * **The UV resistance of the foam:** UV resistance is important for garden furniture because it helps to protect the foam from the sun's harmful rays. Choose a foam that is UV resistant to prevent it from fading or deteriorating over time. * **The ease of cleaning the foam:** You'll want to choose a foam that is easy to clean so that you can keep your furniture looking its best. Look for foams that are stain-resistant and can be easily wiped down with a damp cloth. Once you've considered these factors, you can start to narrow down your choices. Here are a few of the most popular types of foam for garden furniture: * **Polyurethane foam:** Polyurethane foam is a versatile type of foam that is available in a variety of densities and firmnesses. It is also UV resistant and easy to clean. * **Polyethylene foam:** Polyethylene foam is a lightweight and durable type of foam that is also UV resistant. It is a good choice for outdoor furniture that will be exposed to the elements. * **Memory foam:** Memory foam is a type of foam that conforms to your body, providing excellent support and comfort. However, memory foam is not as UV resistant as other types of foam, so it is not the best choice for outdoor furniture that will be exposed to the sun. By following these tips, you can choose the best type of foam for your garden furniture needs.
