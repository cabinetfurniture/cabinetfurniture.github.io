Tips

Here are some tips for choosing and caring for garden furniture:

Tip 1: Choose the right material.

  • Wood: Wood is a classic choice for garden furniture. It is strong, durable, and can be stained or painted to match any décor.

Garden furniture is exposed to the elements, so it is important to protect it from the sun, rain, and wind. You can do this by using covers, cushions, and sealants.
