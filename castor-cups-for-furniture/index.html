Castor cups are small, protective devices that are placed under the legs of furniture to protect both the furniture and the floor. They are typically made of a durable material such as plastic or rubber, and they come in a variety of sizes and shapes to accommodate different types of furniture.

Castor cups serve several important functions. First, they help to prevent the furniture from scratching or damaging the floor. This is especially important for floors made of delicate materials such as hardwood or laminate. Second, castor cups can help to reduce noise by dampening the sound of the furniture moving across the floor. This can be a valuable feature for people who live in apartments or other close-quarters living situations.

In addition to their functional benefits, castor cups can also be used to add a decorative touch to furniture. They are available in a variety of colors and styles, so you can choose the ones that best match your décor.

Easy to install

Castor cups are very easy to install. All you need is a screwdriver and a few minutes of time.

To install castor cups, simply follow these steps:

  1. Turn the furniture upside down.
  2. Align the castor cup with the leg of the furniture.
  3. Screw the castor cup into the leg of the furniture.
  4. Repeat steps 2 and 3 for the remaining legs of the furniture.

Once you have installed the castor cups, turn the furniture right side up and enjoy your new, protected furniture!
