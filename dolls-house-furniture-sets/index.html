Tips

Here are a few practical tips for choosing and caring for garden furniture:

Tip 1: Choose the right material for your needs.

  • Wood:
    Wood is a classic choice for garden furniture. It is strong, durable, and has a natural beauty. However, wood requires regular maintenance, such as staining or sealing, to protect it from the elements.
  • Metal:
    Metal furniture is another popular choice. It is lightweight, durable, and easy to clean. However, metal furniture can get hot in the sun, so it's important to choose a material that is coated to resist heat.
  • Plastic:
    Plastic furniture is a budget-friendly option that is easy to care for. It is also lightweight and weather-resistant. However, plastic furniture can be less durable than other materials.
  • Wicker:
    Wicker furniture is made from natural materials, such as rattan or bamboo. It is lightweight, durable, and has a natural, bohemian look. However, wicker furniture can be more expensive than other materials.
