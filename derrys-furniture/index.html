Tips

Whether you're a seasoned gardener or just starting to explore the joys of outdoor living, Derry's has compiled a few tips to help you create a beautiful and functional garden space.

Tip 1: Choose the right furniture for your needs. Consider the size and layout of your garden, as well as the type of activities you'll be doing in the space. If you plan on entertaining guests, you'll need comfortable seating and a table for dining. If you're looking to relax and unwind, a hammock or chaise lounge may be a better choice.

Tip 2: Use weather-resistant materials. Garden furniture is exposed to the elements, so it's important to choose pieces that are made from durable materials that can withstand rain, sun, and wind. Teak, aluminum, and wicker are all good options for outdoor furniture.

Tip 3: Accessorize with cushions and throws. Cushions and throws can add a touch of comfort and style to your garden furniture. Choose fabrics that are fade-resistant and easy to clean, such as Sunbrella or Olefin.

Tip 4: Create a focal point. Every garden needs a focal point, which could be a water feature, a sculpture, or a beautiful tree. Once you have a focal point, you can arrange your furniture around it to create a cohesive and inviting space.

With these tips in mind, you can create a garden that is both beautiful and functional, a space where you can relax, entertain guests, and enjoy the beauty of nature.
