Have questions about our garden furniture? Here are some frequently asked questions to help you:

Question 1: What materials are your garden furniture made from?
Answer 1: Our garden furniture is made from a variety of high-quality materials, including durable hardwoods, weather-resistant wicker, and powder-coated steel. These materials are chosen for their durability, comfort, and style, ensuring that your furniture will withstand the elements and look great for years to come.

Question 2: How do I care for my garden furniture?
Answer 2: Caring for your garden furniture is easy. Simply clean it with a mild soap and water solution, and dry it thoroughly. For wooden furniture, you may want to apply a sealant or oil to protect it from the elements. For wicker furniture, you can use a soft brush or vacuum cleaner to remove dirt and debris.

Question 3: Can I leave my garden furniture outside all year round?
Answer 3: Yes, our garden furniture is designed to withstand the elements and can be left outside all year round. However, we recommend storing cushions and other accessories indoors during inclement weather.

Question 4: How do I assemble my garden furniture?

Tips

Here are a few practical tips to help you choose the perfect garden furniture for your outdoor space:

1. Consider your space: Before you start shopping, take some time to measure your outdoor space and determine how much furniture you can fit. You don't want to overcrowd your space, but you also want to have enough seating for your guests.

2. Choose the right materials: Garden furniture is exposed to the elements, so it's important to choose materials that can stand up to the weather. Durable hardwoods, weather-resistant wicker, and powder-coated steel are all good options.

3. Think about comfort: You'll be spending a lot of time in your garden furniture, so make sure it's comfortable. Choose chairs and sofas with cushions that are thick and supportive. You may also want to consider adding throw pillows for extra comfort.

4. Don't forget about style: Of course, you want your garden furniture to look good. Choose pieces that match your personal style and the décor of your outdoor space. You can also add your unique touch with cushions, throws, and other accessories.

With these tips in mind, you're sure to find the perfect garden furniture to create a beautiful and comfortable outdoor space.
