Accurate scale

Tudor dolls house furniture is made to an accurate scale, which means that it is a miniature representation of the furniture that was used in real Tudor homes. This allows collectors to create realistic and detailed dolls houses that accurately reflect the homes of the Tudor period.

  • 1:12 scale

    This is the most common scale for Tudor dolls house furniture. It means that the furniture is 1/12th of the size of the real furniture. This scale is popular because it allows collectors to create dolls houses that are large enough to be detailed and realistic, but not so large that they take up too much space.

  • 1:24 scale

    This scale is also known as half-scale. It means that the furniture is 1/24th of the size of the real furniture. This scale is popular for creating smaller dolls houses that are more portable and easier to store.

  • 1:48 scale

    This scale is also known as quarter-scale. It means that the furniture is 1/48th of the size of the real furniture. This scale is popular for creating very small dolls houses that can be displayed in a cabinet or on a shelf.

  • Custom scales

    Some collectors choose to create dolls houses in custom scales. This allows them to create dolls houses that are exactly the size they want. Custom scales are often used for creating very large or very small dolls houses.
