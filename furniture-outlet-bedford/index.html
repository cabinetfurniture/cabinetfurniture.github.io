Answer 1: The best material for garden furniture depends on your individual needs and preferences. Some of the most popular materials include:

  • Cedar: Cedar is another durable hardwood that is resistant to rot and decay. It is also a lightweight wood, making it easy to move around.
  • Wrought iron: Wrought iron is a strong and durable material that is resistant to rust. It is a good choice for outdoor furniture that will be used in a covered area.
  • Aluminum: Aluminum is a lightweight and durable metal that is resistant to rust. It is a good choice for outdoor furniture that will be used in a sunny area.
  • Plastic: Plastic is a lightweight and affordable material that is available in a variety of colors and styles. It is a good choice for outdoor furniture that will be used in a covered area.
