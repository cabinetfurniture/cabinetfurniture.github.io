FAQ

Have questions about caring for your garden furniture? We've compiled a list of frequently asked questions and answers to help you keep your outdoor furniture looking its best.

Question 1: How often should I clean my garden furniture?
Answer 1: Ideally, you should clean your garden furniture at least once a month, or more frequently if it's exposed to harsh weather conditions or heavy use.

Question 2: What's the best way to clean garden furniture?
Answer 2: Use a mild detergent and warm water, and avoid using harsh chemicals or abrasive cleaners. Rinse thoroughly and allow the furniture to dry completely before use.

Question 3: How can I protect my garden furniture from the elements?
Answer 3: Use a weather-resistant sealant or cover to protect your furniture from rain, sun, and other harsh weather conditions.

Question 4: What's the best way to store garden furniture during the off-season?
Answer 4: Clean and dry your furniture thoroughly, and store it in a cool, dry place. Consider using furniture covers or tarps for added protection.

Question 5: How can I remove stubborn stains from my garden furniture?
Answer 5: For stubborn stains, try using a mixture of baking soda and water. Apply the paste to the stain and let it sit for several hours before rinsing it off.

Question 6: What should I do if my garden furniture is starting to rust?
Answer 6: Remove the rust using a wire brush or sandpaper, then apply a rust-resistant primer and paint to protect the metal.

Question 7: How can I keep my garden furniture looking its best?
Answer 7: Regular cleaning and maintenance are key. Avoid leaving cushions and pillows outside when not in use, and consider using a furniture cover to protect your furniture from the elements when not in use.

Closing Paragraph for FAQ: By following these tips and answering these frequently asked questions, you can keep your garden furniture looking its best and extend its lifespan, allowing you to enjoy your outdoor space for years to come.
