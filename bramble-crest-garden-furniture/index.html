Premium Materials

Bramble Crest garden furniture is crafted from premium materials that are carefully selected for their durability, weather resistance, and aesthetic appeal.

  • Teak Wood:

    Bramble Crest uses sustainably sourced teak wood, known for its exceptional strength, durability, and natural resistance to rot, decay, and insects. Teak wood furniture can withstand harsh weather conditions and ages beautifully over time, developing a rich patina that adds to its charm.

  • Stainless Steel:

    Bramble Crest incorporates stainless steel accents into their furniture designs, providing a touch of modern elegance and ensuring resistance to rust and corrosion. Stainless steel is highly durable and easy to maintain, making it an ideal choice for outdoor furniture.

  • All-Weather Wicker:

    Bramble Crest's all-weather wicker is made from high-density polyethylene (HDPE), a synthetic material that is resistant to fading, cracking, and moisture damage. This type of wicker is designed to withstand the elements and maintain its beauty season after season.

  • Sunbrella Fabrics:

    Bramble Crest cushions are upholstered in Sunbrella fabrics, which are renowned for their exceptional resistance to fading, water repellency, and mildew resistance. Sunbrella fabrics are also easy to clean and maintain, ensuring that your cushions stay looking their best for years to come.

By using these premium materials, Bramble Crest garden furniture is designed to provide long-lasting beauty, durability, and comfort in your outdoor space.
