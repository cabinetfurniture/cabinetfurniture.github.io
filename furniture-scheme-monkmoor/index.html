Our showroom on Monkmoor Road offers a wide range of furniture, from sofas and armchairs to beds, wardrobes, and kitchen appliances. We also offer a delivery service for a small fee.

The Monkmoor Furniture Scheme is a vital service for the local community. We help people to furnish their homes with good quality furniture at a fraction of the cost of buying new. We also reduce waste by reusing and recycling preloved furniture.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about garden furniture:

Question 1: What is the best material for garden furniture?
Answer: The best material for garden furniture depends on your needs and preferences. Wood is a classic choice that is durable and stylish, but it requires regular maintenance. Metal is another durable option that is easy to clean, but it can be heavy and hot in the sun. Plastic is a lightweight and affordable option that is available in a variety of colors and styles, but it can be less durable than wood or metal.

Question 2: How do I clean garden furniture?
Answer: The best way to clean garden furniture depends on the material. Wood furniture should be cleaned with a mild soap and water solution and then sealed with a water-resistant finish. Metal furniture can be cleaned with a mild soap and water solution or a commercial metal cleaner. Plastic furniture can be cleaned with a mild soap and water solution or a commercial plastic cleaner.

Question 3: How do I protect garden furniture from the elements?
Answer: The best way to protect garden furniture from the elements is to cover it when it is not in use. You can use a furniture cover, a tarp, or even a plastic sheet. You should also store garden furniture in a dry place during the winter months.

Question 4: How do I repair garden furniture?
Answer: The best way to repair garden furniture depends on the material and the type of damage. Wood furniture can be repaired with wood glue, wood filler, or a wood patch. Metal furniture can be repaired with a metal epoxy or a metal patch. Plastic furniture can be repaired with a plastic epoxy or a plastic patch.

Question 5: How do I dispose of garden furniture?
Answer: The best way to dispose of garden furniture depends on your local regulations. You may be able to donate it to a charity, recycle it, or dispose of it at a landfill.

Question 6: Where can I buy garden furniture?
Answer: You can buy garden furniture at a variety of stores, including home improvement stores, furniture stores, and online retailers.
