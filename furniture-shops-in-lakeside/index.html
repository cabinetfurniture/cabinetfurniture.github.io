FAQ

Have questions about choosing or caring for garden furniture? Here are some frequently asked questions and answers to help you make informed decisions and keep your outdoor furniture looking its best.

Question 1: What materials are best for garden furniture?
Answer 1: The best materials for garden furniture depend on your needs and preferences. Some popular options include:

  • Teak: Known for its durability and resistance to rot and decay, teak is a classic choice for garden furniture.
  • Wicker: Wicker furniture adds a natural and inviting touch to your outdoor space. It is lightweight and easy to move around.
  • Metal: Metal furniture, such as aluminum or wrought iron, is durable and weather-resistant. It is also relatively easy to clean and maintain.
  • Plastic: Plastic furniture is a budget-friendly option that comes in a variety of colors and styles. It is lightweight and easy to clean.

Question 2: How do I choose the right size and style of garden furniture?
Answer 2: Consider the size of your outdoor space and the number of people you typically entertain when choosing garden furniture. You want to choose pieces that are proportionate to the space and that allow for comfortable movement.

Question 3: How do I care for my garden furniture?
Answer 3: Regular cleaning and maintenance will help keep your garden furniture looking its best and extend its lifespan. Clean your furniture with a mild detergent and water, and dry it thoroughly after cleaning. Apply protective finishes or sealants to wooden or metal furniture as needed.

Question 4: How can I protect my garden furniture from the elements?
Answer 4: To protect your garden furniture from sun damage, rain, and other harsh weather conditions, consider using covers or storing the furniture in a sheltered area when not in use.

Question 5: How do I create a cohesive and inviting outdoor space with my garden furniture?
Answer 5: To create a cohesive and inviting outdoor space, choose furniture pieces that complement each other in terms of style and color. Add cushions, throws, and other accessories to add comfort and personality.

Question 6: Where can I find high-quality garden furniture?
Answer 6: Lakeside's furniture shops offer a wide selection of high-quality garden furniture to suit different tastes and budgets. Visit the shops or browse their online catalogs to find the perfect pieces for your outdoor space.

With careful consideration and proper care, your garden furniture can provide years of enjoyment and create a beautiful and inviting outdoor oasis for you and your guests.
