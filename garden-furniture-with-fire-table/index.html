Heat Output: Choose a fire table with a heat output that's appropriate for your climate and desired level of warmth.

The heat output of a fire table is measured in British Thermal Units (BTUs). The higher the BTU rating, the more heat the fire table will produce. When choosing a fire table, it is important to consider your climate and desired level of warmth.

If you live in a cold climate, you'll want to choose a fire table with a high BTU rating. This will ensure that the fire table produces enough heat to keep you warm on even the coldest nights.

If you live in a warm climate, you may prefer a fire table with a lower BTU rating. This will help to prevent the fire table from overheating your outdoor space.

In addition to climate, you should also consider your desired level of warmth when choosing a fire table. If you want to be able to feel the heat from the fire table, you'll want to choose a model with a higher BTU rating. If you prefer a more subtle warmth, you can choose a model with a lower BTU rating.

By considering your climate and desired level of warmth, you can choose a fire table with the perfect heat output for your needs.

    Details:

    • Wood-burning fire tables are a good choice for people who want a traditional fire table experience.
