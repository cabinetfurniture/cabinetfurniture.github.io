Easy to clean and maintain

Another great advantage of faux rattan garden furniture is that it's easy to clean and maintain.

  • Use a mild soap solution for tougher stains

    If you have tougher stains on your faux rattan furniture, you can use a mild soap solution to clean it. Simply mix a few drops of mild dish soap with water and use a soft cloth to wipe down the furniture. Rinse the furniture with clean water afterwards.

    Harsh chemicals can damage faux rattan furniture, so it's important to avoid using them. Stick to mild soap solutions or water when cleaning your furniture.

  • Store your furniture indoors during the winter

    If you live in an area with cold winters, it's a good idea to store your faux rattan furniture indoors during the winter months. This will help to protect it from the elements and extend its lifespan.
