Question 1: What materials are commonly used for garden furniture?
Answer 1: Garden furniture is typically made from a variety of materials, including wood, metal, plastic, and wicker. Each material has its own unique advantages and disadvantages, so it's important to choose the one that best suits your needs and preferences.

Question 2: How can I protect my garden furniture from the elements?
Answer 2: To protect your garden furniture from the elements, consider using covers or storing it in a dry place when not in use. Additionally, choose furniture made from weather-resistant materials, such as teak or aluminum.

Question 3: How often should I clean my garden furniture?
Answer 3: The frequency of cleaning depends on the materials used and the environment in which the furniture is placed. As a general rule, it's a good idea to clean your garden furniture at least once a season, or more often if it's exposed to harsh weather conditions.

Question 4: What are some tips for choosing comfortable garden furniture?
Answer 4: When selecting comfortable garden furniture, consider factors such as the size and shape of the pieces, the firmness of the cushions, and the overall design. Choose furniture that provides good support and is proportionate to your body size.
