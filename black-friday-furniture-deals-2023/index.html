Black Friday Furniture Deals 2023 The Ultimate Guide to Saving Big

Written by Christine Feb 24, 2024 · 14 min read

Are you ready for the biggest shopping event of the year? Black Friday is just around the corner, and it's time to start planning your furniture shopping strategy. This year, we're expecting some incredible Black Friday furniture deals that will allow you to save big on everything from sofas and dining sets to mattresses and bedroom furniture.

Whether you're looking for a few new pieces to refresh your home or you're planning a complete furniture makeover, Black Friday is the perfect time to find what you need at a fraction of the cost. To help you make the most of this shopping extravaganza, we've put together this ultimate guide to Black Friday furniture deals 2023.

With our expert tips and tricks, you'll be able to score the best deals on the furniture you want without breaking the bank. So, grab a cup of coffee, get comfortable, and let's dive into the world of Black Friday furniture deals!

Shop early for best selection.

One of the most important tips for scoring the best Black Friday furniture deals is to shop early. The best deals are typically available on the first day of the sale, and the selection is often much wider. If you wait until later in the sale, you may find that the best items are already gone, and you may not be able to find the perfect piece of furniture for your home.

If you can, try to shop on Black Friday itself. However, if you're unable to make it out on that day, many stores will extend their Black Friday sales for a few days or even a week. This will give you a chance to shop the sale without having to deal with the huge Black Friday early morning rush.

Another option is to shop online. Many online furniture stores participate in Black Friday, and they often offer exclusive online-only deals. This can be a great way to avoid the hassle of shopping in-store. Just be sure to check the shipping and return policies of the online store before you make a purchase.

No matter how you choose to shop, be sure to do your research before the sale starts. This will help you know which pieces of furniture you're interested in and which stores are offering the best deals. You can also sign up for email alerts from your favorite furniture stores to be the first to know about their Black Friday sales.

Shop early for best selection.

Once you know which pieces of furniture you're interested in, it's time to start comparing prices from multiple stores. This is the best way to make sure you're getting the best deal possible.

  • Check online price comparison websites.

    There are a number of websites that allow you to compare prices from multiple stores at the same time. This can be a great way to find the best deal on the furniture you want.

  • Visit multiple furniture stores.

    If you have the time, it's a good idea to visit multiple furniture stores to compare prices. This will give you a good sense of the average price of the furniture you're interested in. You may also be able to find some hidden gems at smaller, independent furniture stores.

  • Ask for a price match.

    Many furniture stores will offer a price match if you find the same item for a lower price at another store. This is a great way to get the best possible deal on the furniture you want.

  • Consider buying used furniture.

    If you're on a tight budget, you may want to consider buying used furniture. There are a number of websites and stores that sell used furniture in good condition. You can also find used furniture at thrift stores and garage sales.

By comparing prices from multiple stores, you can be sure that you're getting the best deal possible on your Black Friday furniture purchases.

Look for bundle deals and discounts.

Many furniture stores offer bundle deals and discounts on Black Friday. This is a great way to save even more money on your purchase. Here are a few things to look for:

  • Furniture sets.

    Buying a furniture set is often cheaper than buying each piece individually. This is especially true for bedroom sets, dining room sets, and living room sets.

  • Mix and match discounts.

    Some stores offer discounts when you mix and match different pieces of furniture. For example, you may get a discount if you buy a sofa and a loveseat together.

  • Free shipping and assembly.

    Some stores offer free shipping and assembly on Black Friday purchases. This can save you a lot of money, especially if you're buying large or heavy furniture.

  • Loyalty programs.

    If you're a member of a furniture store's loyalty program, you may be eligible for additional discounts on Black Friday. Be sure to ask about loyalty program discounts before you make your purchase.

By taking advantage of bundle deals and discounts, you can save even more money on your Black Friday furniture purchases.

Don't forget about online retailers.

In addition to brick-and-mortar stores, many online retailers also offer Black Friday furniture deals. This can be a great option if you prefer to shop from the comfort of your own home or if you're looking for a wider selection of furniture.

  • Check major online retailers.

    Major online retailers like Amazon, Wayfair, and Overstock often have great Black Friday furniture deals. Be sure to check their websites and apps for the latest deals.

  • Visit online furniture stores.

    There are also a number of online furniture stores that offer Black Friday deals. These stores often specialize in a particular type of furniture, such as mid-century modern furniture or Scandinavian furniture. You can find some great deals on unique and stylish furniture at these stores.

  • Read reviews before you buy.

    When shopping for furniture online, it's important to read reviews before you buy. This will help you make sure that you're getting a quality product. You can find reviews on websites like Amazon, Wayfair, and Overstock.

  • Be aware of shipping costs.

    When shopping for furniture online, be sure to factor in the cost of shipping. Shipping costs can vary depending on the size and weight of the furniture you're purchasing. Some stores offer free shipping on orders over a certain amount, so be sure to take advantage of these offers.

By shopping online, you can find a wider selection of furniture and great deals on Black Friday. Just be sure to read reviews and factor in the cost of shipping before you buy.

Read reviews before you buy.

When shopping for furniture online, it's important to read reviews before you buy. This will help you make sure that you're getting a quality product and that you're not wasting your money.

There are a few things to look for when reading reviews:

  • Overall rating.

    The overall rating of a product is a good place to start. This will give you a general idea of how other customers have liked the product.

  • Number of reviews.

    The number of reviews is also important. The more reviews a product has, the more accurate the overall rating is likely to be.

  • Individual reviews.

    Take some time to read individual reviews to get a better idea of what other customers have liked and disliked about the product. Pay attention to reviews that mention the quality of the materials, the construction of the furniture, and the customer service of the store.

  • Verified purchases.

    When possible, try to read reviews from customers who have verified purchases. This means that they have actually bought the product and are not just leaving a fake review.

By reading reviews, you can make sure that you're buying a quality product that you'll be happy with for years to come.

Here are some additional tips for reading reviews:

  • Be skeptical of reviews that are too positive or too negative.

    It's possible that these reviews are fake.

  • Pay attention to the date of the reviews.

    Reviews from older customers may not be as relevant as reviews from recent customers.

  • Consider the source of the reviews.

    Some websites are more reliable than others when it comes to reviews.

Be prepared for crowds and long lines.

Black Friday is one of the busiest shopping days of the year, so it's important to be prepared for crowds and long lines. Here are a few tips:

  • Arrive early.

    If you're planning to shop on Black Friday, it's a good idea to arrive early. This will help you avoid the worst of the crowds.

  • Dress comfortably.

    You'll be doing a lot of walking and standing, so be sure to dress comfortably. Wear shoes that are easy to walk in and clothes that you can move around easily in.

  • Bring snacks and drinks.

    You may have to wait in line for a while, so it's a good idea to bring some snacks and drinks with you. This will help you stay energized and hydrated.

  • Be patient.

    Black Friday can be a hectic and stressful day, so it's important to be patient. Remember that the store employees are doing their best to help you.

If you're shopping online, you can avoid the crowds and long lines altogether. However, you may have to pay shipping costs, and you may not be able to get your furniture as quickly as you would if you shopped in-store.

No matter how you choose to shop on Black Friday, be prepared for crowds and long lines. With a little planning and patience, you can make the most of this shopping holiday and get the furniture you want at a great price.

Enjoy your new furniture!

After all the hustle and bustle of Black Friday, it's time to relax and enjoy your new furniture. Here are a few tips:

  • Assemble your furniture carefully.

    If your furniture requires assembly, be sure to follow the instructions carefully. This will help you avoid any problems.

  • Place your furniture in the right spot.

    Take some time to think about where you want to place your new furniture. Make sure it's in a spot where it's functional and where it looks its best.

  • Accessorize your furniture.

    Add some pillows, throws, and other accessories to your new furniture to make it your own. This will help it feel like part of your home.

  • Take care of your furniture.

    To keep your new furniture looking its best, be sure to clean it regularly. You should also avoid placing it in direct sunlight or near heat sources.

By following these tips, you can enjoy your new furniture for years to come.

Black Friday is a great time to save money on furniture, but it's important to remember that the best deals are often available to those who are prepared. By following the tips in this article, you can make the most of Black Friday and get the furniture you want at a great price.
