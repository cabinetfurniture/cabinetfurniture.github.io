Rust-resistant and corrosion-resistant

Cast aluminium outdoor furniture is highly resistant to rust and corrosion, making it an excellent choice for outdoor use. Unlike iron or steel, which are prone to rusting when exposed to moisture, cast aluminium forms a protective oxide layer on its surface that prevents the metal from corroding.

This oxide layer is self-healing, meaning that even if the furniture is scratched or damaged, the protective layer will quickly reform, preventing rust and corrosion from spreading.

The corrosion resistance of cast aluminium is further enhanced by the powder coating that is often applied to outdoor furniture. Powder coating is a process in which a dry powder is electrostatically applied to the furniture and then cured under heat. This creates a durable and weather-resistant finish that provides an additional barrier against moisture and other corrosive elements.

As a result of its exceptional rust and corrosion resistance, cast aluminium outdoor furniture can withstand even the harshest weather conditions, including rain, snow, and saltwater exposure. This makes it an ideal choice for coastal areas or other environments where rust and corrosion are a concern.

By choosing cast aluminium outdoor furniture, you can enjoy the peace of mind that comes with knowing that your furniture will remain rust-free and corrosion-resistant for many years to come.
