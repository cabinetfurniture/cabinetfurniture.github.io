Durable materials

One of the best things about MD garden furniture is that it is made from durable materials that are built to last.

  • Teak

    Teak is a naturally durable hardwood that is resistant to rot, decay, and insects. It is also a very strong wood, making it ideal for outdoor furniture.

  • Stainless steel

    Stainless steel is another durable material that is resistant to rust and corrosion. It is also a very strong material, making it ideal for outdoor furniture.

  • Aluminum

    Aluminum is a lightweight and durable metal that is resistant to rust and corrosion. It is also a very strong material, making it ideal for outdoor furniture.

  • Polyethylene

    Polyethylene is a durable plastic that is resistant to UV rays and fading. It is also a very strong material, making it ideal for outdoor furniture.

Thanks to these durable materials, MD garden furniture can withstand the elements and last for many years to come.
