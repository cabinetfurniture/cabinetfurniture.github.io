Select comfortable sitting

When choosing garden furniture, comfort should be one of your top considerations. After all, you want to be able to relax and enjoy your outdoor space without having to suffer from back pain or other discomfort.

  • Choose furniture with good back support. When you're sitting in a chair, your back should be supported in a way that keeps your posture and simultaneously provides a sense of comfort.
  • Choose furniture with a comfortable seat. The seat of your chair should be firm enough to provide support, but not so firm that it's like sitting on a rock. It should also be wide enough to allow you to move around comfortably.
  • Choose furniture with armrests. Armrests can help to reduce strain on your shoulders and neck, especially if you're sitting in the chair for long periods of time. This is especially significant for the elderly or those with joint pain.
  • Consider the height of the furniture. The height of your furniture should be comfortable for you to get in and out of, and it should also be proportionate to the height of your table or other furniture pieces.

By following these tips, you can choose garden furniture that is both stylish and comfortable, allowing you to fully enjoy your outdoor space.

Add some shade

Dubai's climate is hot and sunny for most of the year, so it's important to add some shade to your garden if you want to enjoy it during the day. There are a few different ways to do this:

  • Use a parasol. Parasols are a great way to add shade to a small area, such as a seating area or dining table. They are easy to move around and can be adjusted to provide shade at different times of the day.
  • Install a pergola. Pergolas are permanent structures that provide shade over a larger area. They can be made from a variety of materials, such as wood, metal, or fabric. Pergolas can be attached to your house or freestanding.
  • Plant trees or shrubs. Trees and shrubs can provide natural shade for your garden. They can also help to cool the air and reduce humidity. When choosing trees or shrubs for shade, be sure to choose varieties that are native to Dubai's climate.
  • Use shade sails. Shade sails are large pieces of fabric that are suspended over a seating area or other area that you want to shade. They are a great way to add shade without having to install a permanent structure.

By adding some shade to your garden, you can create a more comfortable and enjoyable space to relax and entertain.
