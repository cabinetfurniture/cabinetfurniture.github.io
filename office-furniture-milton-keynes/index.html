Comfort: Employees should be comfortable in their chairs and at their desks, so it's important to choose furniture that is ergonomically designed.

Employees spend a lot of time sitting in their chairs and at their desks, so it's important to choose furniture that is comfortable. Ergonomic furniture is designed to support the body and reduce the risk of pain and discomfort.

Choose chairs with adjustable seats and backs.

Adjustable chairs allow employees to customize their seating position to find the most comfortable position. Look for chairs with seats that adjust up and down, and backs that adjust forward and back.

Make sure the desks are the right height.

Provide footrests and lumbar supports.

Footrests and lumbar supports can help to improve comfort and reduce pain. Footrests help to keep feet elevated, which can reduce leg fatigue. Lumbar supports help to support the lower back, which can reduce back pain.
