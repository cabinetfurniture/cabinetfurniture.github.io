Wood

Metal

Easy to clean and maintain

Furniture-style dog crates are easy to clean and maintain. This is important because you will need to clean your dog's crate regularly to prevent the buildup of bacteria and dirt. A clean crate will also help to keep your dog healthy and comfortable.

Removable trays

Many furniture-style dog crates come with removable trays that make cleaning a breeze. Simply remove the tray and wash it with soap and water. You can also place the tray in the dishwasher for a more thorough cleaning.

Wipe-clean surfaces

Most furniture-style dog crates have wipe-clean surfaces. This means that you can simply wipe down the crate with a damp cloth to clean it. This is a great way to remove dirt and debris from the crate.

Ventilation panels

Ventilation panels help to keep the air inside the crate fresh and circulating. This helps to prevent the buildup of odors and bacteria. Ventilation panels also help to keep your dog cool and comfortable.
