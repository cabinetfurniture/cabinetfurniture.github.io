Rent-to-own stores

Rent-to-own stores allow you to rent furniture for a period of time, typically 12 to 24 months, with the option to purchase the furniture at the end of the rental period. This is a good option if you don't have the money to purchase furniture outright, or if you're not sure if you'll want to keep the furniture for a long period of time.

  • No credit check: Rent-to-own stores do not typically require a credit check, which makes them a good option for people with bad credit or no credit history.
  • Easy approval: Rent-to-own stores typically have very easy approval processes, so you can get the furniture you need quickly and easily.
  • Flexible payment options: Rent-to-own stores offer a variety of payment options, so you can choose a plan that fits your budget.
  • Build credit: Some rent-to-own stores report your rental payments to the credit bureaus, which can help you build credit over time.

It's important to note that rent-to-own stores can be more expensive than other options, so it's important to compare prices before you make a purchase. You should also read the rental agreement carefully before you sign it, so you understand all of the terms and conditions.

You should also ask the store about their return policy, in case you're not satisfied with your purchase.
