Choose the right type of rattan

There are two main types of rattan used in garden furniture: natural rattan and synthetic rattan.

  • Natural rattan is made from the stems of the rattan palm, and it is a strong and durable material. However, it is also more expensive than synthetic rattan and requires more maintenance.
  • Synthetic rattan is made from polyethylene or PVC, and it is designed to look like natural rattan. It is less expensive than natural rattan and requires less maintenance, but it is not as strong or durable.

When choosing between natural and synthetic rattan, consider the following factors:

  • Budget: Natural rattan is more expensive than synthetic rattan.
  • Durability: Natural rattan is more durable than synthetic rattan.
  • Maintenance: Natural rattan requires more maintenance than synthetic rattan.
  • Style: Both natural and synthetic rattan can be used to create a variety of styles.

Consider the size of your space

When choosing rattan garden furniture, it is important to consider the size of your space. You want to make sure that the furniture is proportional to the size of your space and that it does not overwhelm the area.

If you have a small space, you will want to choose smaller pieces of furniture. A love seat or a set of chairs with a small table would be a good option. You may also want to consider hanging furniture, such as a swing or a hammock, to save space.

If you have a large space, you can choose larger pieces of furniture. A sectional sofa or a dining table with chairs would be a good option. You can also add some accent pieces, such as a coffee table or a side table, to complete the look.

No matter what size your space is, you can find rattan garden furniture that will fit your needs. Just be sure to measure your space before you start shopping so that you can choose the right size furniture.

Think about the style you want

Rattan garden furniture comes in a variety of styles, from traditional to modern. When choosing a style, consider the overall look and feel of your outdoor space. Do you want a classic look? A more contemporary look? Or something in between?

If you have a traditional home, you may want to choose rattan furniture with a classic design. This could include pieces with curved lines and intricate details. If you have a more modern home, you may prefer rattan furniture with a more streamlined design. This could include pieces with clean lines and simple shapes.

Of course, you can also mix and match different styles to create your own unique look. For example, you could pair a traditional rattan sofa with a modern coffee table. Or, you could add some contemporary throw pillows to a classic rattan chair.

No matter what style you choose, be sure to select pieces that you love and that will complement your existing décor.

Order a sample before buying in bulk

If you are unsure about the quality of the rattan garden furniture, it is a good idea to order a sample before buying in bulk. This will allow you to inspect the furniture closely and make sure that it meets your expectations.

  • Most suppliers will be willing to send you a sample of their furniture. However, you may have to pay a small fee to cover the shipping costs.
  • When ordering a sample, be sure to specify the type of furniture you want, the size, and the color. You should also ask the supplier to send you a sample of the fabric or cushion material.
  • Once you receive the sample, inspect it carefully. Look for any defects in the craftsmanship or materials. Also, make sure that the furniture is the right size and style for your space.
  • If you are not satisfied with the sample, you can return it to the supplier for a refund. However, some suppliers may charge a restocking fee.

Ordering a sample before buying in bulk is a good way to protect yourself from getting stuck with furniture that you are not happy with.

Inspect the furniture carefully before accepting delivery

Once your rattan garden furniture arrives, it is important to inspect it carefully before accepting delivery. This will help you to identify any defects or damage that may have occurred during shipping.

Here are a few things to look for when inspecting your furniture:

  • Check for any cracks or splits in the rattan. These could be a sign of damage or poor craftsmanship.
  • Make sure that all of the joints are tight and secure. Loose joints can cause the furniture to become unstable or collapse.
  • Inspect the fabric or cushion material for any tears or stains. You should also make sure that the fabric is properly attached to the furniture.
  • Check the hardware to make sure that it is all present and accounted for. Missing or damaged hardware can make it difficult to assemble the furniture.

If you find any defects or damage, you should refuse delivery and contact the supplier immediately. They will be able to send you a replacement or refund your money.

Follow the care instructions to keep your furniture looking its best

To keep your rattan garden furniture looking its best, it is important to follow the care instructions provided by the manufacturer. These instructions will vary depending on the type of rattan and finish used.

In general, rattan furniture should be cleaned regularly with a soft brush or cloth. You can also use a mild soap and water solution to remove dirt and grime. Be sure to rinse the furniture thoroughly with clean water after cleaning.

If your rattan furniture is exposed to the elements, it is important to protect it with a sealant or finish. This will help to prevent the rattan from fading or cracking. You should also store your furniture indoors during the winter months to protect it from the cold and snow.

By following the care instructions, you can keep your rattan garden furniture looking its best for many years to come.
