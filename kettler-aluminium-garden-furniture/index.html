Durable and weather-resistant

Kettler aluminium garden furniture is renowned for its exceptional durability and weather resistance, ensuring years of enjoyment in any outdoor environment.

  • Rust-proof and corrosion-resistant: Aluminium's natural oxide layer protects it from rust and corrosion, making it ideal for outdoor use, even in harsh coastal or humid environments.
  • UV-resistant: Kettler's aluminium furniture is treated with a special powder coating that protects it from the sun's harmful UV rays, preventing fading and discolouration, ensuring that your furniture retains its vibrant appearance season after season.
  • Extreme temperature resistance: Aluminium can withstand extreme temperatures without warping or cracking, making it suitable for use in both hot and cold climates. It can endure freezing winters and scorching summers without compromising its structural integrity.
  • Impact-resistant: Aluminium is a robust material that can withstand accidental impacts and knocks, making it ideal for families with children or pets.

The exceptional durability and weather resistance of Kettler aluminium garden furniture make it a worthwhile investment, providing years of enjoyment and eliminating the need for frequent replacements or repairs.

Lightweight and easy to move

Kettler aluminium garden furniture is incredibly lightweight and easy to move, making it a breeze to rearrange your outdoor space or store it away when needed.

  • Effortless rearrangement: The lightweight nature of aluminium allows you to effortlessly move chairs, tables, and sofas around your outdoor area to create different configurations and accommodate your changing needs.
  • Convenient storage: When it's time to pack away your furniture for the season or during inclement weather, the lightweight design makes it easy to lift and store, saving you time and effort.
  • No heavy lifting: Unlike traditional iron or wooden furniture, Kettler aluminium furniture can be easily carried by one person, eliminating the need for strenuous lifting or the assistance of others.
  • Gentle on your surfaces: The lightweight design ensures that your furniture won't damage delicate surfaces like decking or patio tiles when moved.

The lightweight and easy-to-move feature of Kettler aluminium garden furniture adds to its overall functionality and convenience, making it a pleasure to use and maintain.

Low maintenance and easy to clean

Kettler aluminium garden furniture is incredibly low maintenance and easy to clean, ensuring that you can spend more time enjoying your outdoor space and less time on upkeep.

  • Wipe-clean surfaces: The smooth, powder-coated surfaces of Kettler aluminium furniture can be easily wiped clean with a damp cloth, removing dirt, dust, and spills effortlessly.

  • Minimal fading: The high-quality powder coating protects the furniture from UV rays, preventing fading and discolouration, ensuring that your furniture retains its vibrant appearance for years to come.
  • No need for covers: Kettler aluminium garden furniture can withstand the elements without the need for protective covers, saving you time and effort.

The low maintenance and easy-to-clean features of Kettler aluminium garden furniture make it an ideal choice for busy individuals and families who want to enjoy their outdoor space without the hassle of constant cleaning and maintenance.

Tips

Here are some practical tips to help you choose and care for your garden furniture:

Tip 1: Consider your needs. Before you start shopping, think about how you'll be using your garden furniture. Will it be used for dining, relaxing, or both? How many people will you need to accommodate? Once you know your needs, you can start to narrow down your choices.

Tip 2: Choose durable materials. Garden furniture is exposed to the elements, so it's important to choose materials that can withstand the sun, rain, and wind. Aluminium, teak, and wicker are all good choices for durable garden furniture.

Tip 3: Pay attention to comfort. You'll be spending a lot of time in your garden furniture, so it's important to make sure it's comfortable. Look for furniture with supportive cushions and ergonomic design.

Tip 4: Don't forget about style. Garden furniture should be both functional and stylish. Choose pieces that complement your outdoor décor and create a cohesive look.

By following these tips, you can choose and care for garden furniture that will provide you with years of enjoyment.
