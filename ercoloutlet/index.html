Question 1: What factors should I consider when choosing garden furniture?
Answer 1: When choosing garden furniture, consider the following factors: - Durability: Choose furniture made from durable materials that can withstand outdoor elements like rain, sun, and wind. - Comfort: Make sure the furniture is comfortable to sit or lie on, especially if you plan to spend long hours relaxing in your garden. - Style: Choose furniture that matches your personal style and the overall aesthetic of your garden. - Functionality: Consider the functionality you need, such as chairs, tables, loungers, or a combination of all three.

Question 2: What are the different types of garden furniture materials?
Answer 2: Common garden furniture materials include: - Wood: Wood furniture is classic and durable, but it requires regular maintenance. - Metal: Metal furniture is sturdy and easy to clean, but it can get hot in the sun. - Plastic: Plastic furniture is lightweight and low-maintenance, but it may not be as durable as other materials. - Wicker: Wicker furniture is natural-looking and comfortable, but it can be susceptible to moisture damage.
