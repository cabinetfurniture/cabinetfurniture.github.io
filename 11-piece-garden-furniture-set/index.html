Size: Make sure the set is the right size for your space.

Before you start shopping for an 11 piece garden furniture set, it is important to measure the space where you plan to put it. This will help you determine what size set will fit comfortably.

  • Measure the length and width of the space. This will give you the overall dimensions of the area where you will be placing the set.
  • Consider the traffic flow around the set. You will need to leave enough space for people to walk around the set without bumping into it.
  • Think about how you will be using the set. If you plan on using it for dining, you will need to make sure there is enough space for everyone to sit comfortably. If you plan on using it for relaxing, you may want to choose a set with larger chairs or a chaise lounge.
  • Don't forget to measure the height of the set. You will need to make sure that the set is not too tall for the space or that it will not block your view.

By following these tips, you can choose an 11 piece garden furniture set that is the right size for your space and your needs.

Material: Choose a material that is durable and weather-resistant.

The material of your 11 piece garden furniture set is an important consideration, as it will affect the durability, weather resistance, and overall appearance of the set. Here are some of the most popular materials used for garden furniture sets:

Wood: Wood is a classic choice for garden furniture, and for good reason. It is durable, weather-resistant, and can be stained or painted to match any dÃ©cor. However, wood does require some maintenance, such as sealing or staining, to protect it from the elements.

Metal: Metal is another popular choice for garden furniture, as it is strong, durable, and weather-resistant. Metal sets are also relatively easy to care for, as they can simply be wiped down with a damp cloth. However, metal sets can be heavy and may rust if not properly cared for.

Rattan: Rattan is a natural material that is made from the stems of the rattan palm. Rattan furniture is lightweight, weather-resistant, and comes in a variety of colors and patterns. However, rattan furniture can be more expensive than other materials.

Plastic: Plastic is a budget-friendly option for garden furniture. It is lightweight, weather-resistant, and easy to care for. However, plastic furniture can be less durable than other materials and may fade over time.

When choosing a material for your 11 piece garden furniture set, consider your budget, the climate in your area, and the amount of maintenance you are willing to do. With proper care, any of these materials can provide years of enjoyment.
