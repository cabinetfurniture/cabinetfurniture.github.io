Sustainable materials

Sustainability is at the core of Helsinki garden furniture, with a strong focus on using environmentally friendly materials and production processes.

  • Teak and acacia wood: Helsinki garden furniture is often made from teak and acacia wood, which are known for their durability, weather resistance, and natural beauty. These woods are sourced from sustainably managed forests, ensuring that they are harvested in a way that minimizes environmental impact.
  • Aluminum: Aluminum is another popular material used in Helsinki garden furniture. It is lightweight, rust-resistant, and recyclable, making it a sustainable choice. Aluminum frames are often powder-coated for added durability and to reduce the need for maintenance.
  • Recycled materials: Helsinki garden furniture also incorporates recycled materials, such as plastic and fabric, to reduce waste and promote circularity. Recycled materials are used in a variety of components, such as cushions, upholstery, and even the frames of some furniture pieces.
  • FSC certification: Helsinki garden furniture manufacturers often seek FSC certification, which ensures that the wood used in their products comes from responsibly managed forests. FSC certification helps to protect forests and promote sustainable forestry practices.

By using sustainable materials and practices, Helsinki garden furniture contributes to a greener and healthier environment, while also ensuring that your outdoor furniture will last for many years to come.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about garden furniture:

Question 1: How do I choose the right garden furniture?
Answer 1: When choosing garden furniture, consider the size and style of your outdoor space, the materials you prefer, and your budget. Think about how you will use the furniture and choose pieces that are comfortable and functional.

Question 2: What are the most durable materials for garden furniture?
Answer 2: Teak, acacia wood, and aluminum are all durable materials for garden furniture. They are resistant to rot, decay, and insects, and they can withstand the elements without warping, cracking, or fading.

Question 3: How do I care for my garden furniture?
Answer 3: To care for your garden furniture, clean it regularly with a mild soap and water solution. You can also use a teak cleaner or protectant to help preserve the wood. Store your furniture in a dry place when not in use.

Question 4: How long will garden furniture last?
Answer 4: The lifespan of garden furniture depends on the materials used and the care it receives. With proper care, high-quality garden furniture can last for many years.

Question 5: Can I leave my garden furniture outside year-round?
Answer 5: If your garden furniture is made from durable materials, such as teak, acacia wood, or aluminum, you can leave it outside year-round. However, it is important to clean and store your furniture properly when not in use.

Question 6: How do I protect my garden furniture from the elements?
Answer 6: To protect your garden furniture from the elements, clean it regularly, apply a protectant or sealant, and store it in a dry place when not in use. You can also use covers to protect your furniture from rain, snow, and wind.

Question 7: How do I choose cushions for my garden furniture?
Answer 7: When choosing cushions for your garden furniture, consider the style of your furniture, the climate you live in, and your personal preferences. Choose cushions that are made from durable materials and that are easy to clean and care for.

These are just a few frequently asked questions about garden furniture. If you have any other questions, please feel free to contact a garden furniture expert.

Tips

Here are a few tips to help you choose the right garden furniture and care for it properly:

Tip 1: Choose the right materials. The materials you choose for your garden furniture will affect its durability, comfort, and appearance. Teak, acacia wood, and aluminum are all durable materials that can withstand the elements. If you prefer a more natural look, you may want to choose wicker or rattan.

Tip 2: Consider your climate. If you live in a climate with extreme temperatures or precipitation, you will need to choose furniture that is designed to withstand those conditions. For example, teak and acacia wood are both naturally resistant to rot and decay, making them a good choice for humid climates. Aluminum is a good choice for coastal areas, as it is resistant to salt corrosion.

Tip 3: Choose comfortable furniture. You will be spending a lot of time in your garden furniture, so it is important to choose pieces that are comfortable. Look for chairs and sofas with supportive cushions and backrests. If you plan on doing a lot of dining outdoors, choose a table that is the right height for your chairs.

Tip 4: Protect your furniture. To extend the lifespan of your garden furniture, it is important to protect it from the elements. Clean your furniture regularly with a mild soap and water solution. You can also use a teak cleaner or protectant to help preserve the wood. Store your furniture in a dry place when not in use.

By following these tips, you can choose the right garden furniture for your needs and enjoy it for many years to come.
